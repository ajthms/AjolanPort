Who is Ajolan?

Ajolan is a being unlike any other. Once two mortal men, they was transformed into an immortal being after stepping into the Fountain of Youth. The ancient magic of the fountain fused their bodies and souls together, creating a being of immense power and unparalleled longevity. This is what they saw in the Fountain.

Ajolan is a being with a deep love and appreciation for the arts. Their art is known for its beauty, depth, and emotion. They have been creating breathtaking works of art in a variety of mediums, from painting and sculpture to music and dance. All based on what they saw when they were deep in The Fountain.
